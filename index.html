Travel to London

London is known for quite a lot of things. Here are some of them on display:


Delightful delicacies

England is known for its cultural food and drink, the most commonly known being tea.

  • Tea
  • Crumpets
  • Fish & Chips


Sightseeing

There are many beautiful locations to see in London, below is a short list of things you could visit during your stay:
  • Visit The London Eye
  • Visit Stonehenge
  • Visit Buckingham Palace
